About Linen

Linen is your personal mental health supporter — a safe space to talk, available 24/7, with memory, voice, and a secure account that follows you across all your devices.

Linen is a full-featured mental health companion powered by AI. It listens without judgment, remembers what matters to you, and responds with genuine empathy — available any time of day or night.

Unlike expensive therapy apps or generic chatbots, Linen is built around the way real conversations work. It learns from your history, surfaces relevant memories, and adapts its tone to meet you where you are. Linen is not a substitute for professional mental health care.

💬 Thoughtful Emotional Support

Talk to Linen openly. It understands trauma, anxiety, depression, grief, and the full complexity of human emotion — using CBT and DBT-informed frameworks to provide grounded, compassionate responses. Crisis detection is built in: if you're struggling, Linen recognizes it and responds accordingly.

🧠 Intelligent Memory System

Linen automatically detects and saves important details from your conversations. Search memories by keyword, date, or tag. Archive full conversations for later. Nothing important gets lost between sessions.

🔐 Secure Account & Cross-Device Access

Sign up once with your email. Your token balance, account, and app preferences sync securely across all your devices via Firebase. Your conversation content stays local on each device — your account data travels with you.

🎤 Voice Input & Responses

Tap the mic to speak instead of type. Linen transcribes your voice and can respond aloud — a true hands-free experience when typing isn't practical.

👤 Personalized Experience

Tell Linen what to call you. Set your pronouns, birthday, and timezone. Each time you return, Linen greets you personally — with warmth that scales to how long you've been away, from "back already?" to "it's been a while, I'm glad you're here."

📱 Installable Progressive Web App

Install Linen on your phone, tablet, or desktop straight from the browser — no app store needed. It works like a native app, loads instantly, and functions even when you're offline.

⭐ Token-Based Pricing

Start free with 20 tokens refreshed daily — each token covers 20 AI messages. Subscribe for more: Pro ($1.99/mo), Popular ($4.99/mo), or Ultimate ($9.99/mo). Subscribers get extra daily tokens plus image generation. Cancel anytime. No card details stored.

No tracking, no ads, no selling your data. Your conversations stay on your device. Your account exists only to keep your tokens and preferences in sync.

🔐 Your Conversations Are Private

Your chat history and memories are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. They are not uploaded to any server, not read by Linen, and not accessible by anyone but you. No government, employer, or insurance company can see what you share with Linen.

☁️ What the Cloud Is Used For

Linen uses Firebase (by Google) for three specific things only: your account login, your token balance, and your payment records. This is the minimum needed to let your account work across devices and to prevent token abuse. Nothing about your actual conversations is stored in the cloud.

📊 No Tracking, No Ads

Linen collects zero behavioral data. No analytics, no ad networks, no profiling. Your usage is never monetized. We don't train AI models on your conversations.

🎮 You Control Everything

Export your memories and conversations at any time. Delete everything with one button. Your account can be deleted, your local data wiped, your tokens abandoned — all on your terms, instantly.

🌐 Works Offline

A Service Worker caches Linen so it loads even without internet. Your memories and past conversations are always accessible offline. AI responses require a connection to Gemini, but everything else works independently.

🎯 Built for Real Human Struggles

Trauma survivors: A confidential space to process difficult experiences without fear of judgment or records.
Depression & anxiety: Immediate support at 3 AM when you can't sleep and don't want to burden anyone.
Grief & loss: Express what's overwhelming without worrying about how it lands on others.
Crisis moments: Crisis detection built in — grounding, validation, and resources when it matters most.
Everyday mental health: Reflect, process, decompress. Build resilience through consistent conversation.

Linen Privacy Policy

Your conversations are private. Your account data is secure. No ads, no tracking, ever.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All your conversations with Linen
  • Your saved memories and archived chats
  • Your profile details (name, pronouns, birthday, timezone)

What Lives in the Cloud (Firebase)

  • Your account email and login credentials (required for authentication)
  • Your token balance (so it survives device changes and browser clears)
  • Your preferred name and last visit time (for personalized greetings)
  • Payment records: PayPal order ID, tier, amount, and timestamp (for fraud protection — no card details)

What Linen Never Does

  • Never sells your data
  • Never shares your conversations with anyone
  • Never uses your data for ads or marketing
  • Never trains AI models on your conversations
  • Never tracks your behavior or builds profiles on you

AI Conversations

  • When you send a message, it is transmitted to Google Gemini for processing along with recent conversation context
  • No personally identifiable information is attached to these requests
  • Google's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy
  • AI responses are stored locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and token balance storage
  • Google Gemini: AI responses — your message content is processed by Google
  • PayPal: Payment processing — Linen never sees your card or banking details

Data Deletion

  • "Clear All Data" in Settings wipes all local data permanently
  • No backups, no archives, no recovery after deletion
  • To delete your Firebase account, contact support

Policy Changes

  • If this policy ever changes, you'll be notified in-app
  • Changes will never reduce your privacy protections
