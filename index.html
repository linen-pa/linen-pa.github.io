What device are you using?

Android (Chrome)

  1. In Chrome, tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. A popup will appear—tap the "Add" button
  4. Linen will appear on your home screen as an app icon

iOS (Safari)

  1. In Safari, tap the Share button (looks like a box with an arrow ⬆️) at the bottom of your screen
  2. Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. You can customize the app name if you want
  4. Tap the "Add" button in the top-right corner
  5. Linen will appear on your home screen as an app icon

Tip: You can also use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge instead of Safari—they all work the same way!

About Linen

Linen is your personal mental health supporter—your safe space to talk about what's on your mind, available 24/7, completely private, and affordable.

Linen is a sophisticated web application designed to be your personal mental health supporter. It learns from your conversations, remembers what matters to you, and provides thoughtful, empathetic responses when you need them most.

Unlike traditional mental health services (expensive, hard to schedule, limited hours) or generic chat apps (track your data, don't understand context), Linen is designed to listen and understand. It engages in real conversations with genuine empathy, supports you through difficult times, and preserves everything you share—completely private, completely yours.

What if you had access to an always-available space to talk about what's on your mind—24/7/365, completely private, and affordable? That's Linen.

💬 Thoughtful Emotional Support

Talk to Linen with complete transparency and vulnerability. Get responses from a genuinely intelligent AI trained to understand trauma, anxiety, depression, and complex emotions. Linen listens with genuine empathy, provides validation and support—without judgment, without cost barriers. Available instantly, anytime you're struggling, completely confidential. Linen is not a substitute for professional mental health care.

🧠 Intelligent Memory System

Linen learns important details from your conversations automatically—no manual input needed. Tag memories for easy searching, find them by keyword, date, or tags, and see emotional context for each memory. Build a personal knowledge base of your life that actually understands you.

🗓️ Calendar & Reminders

Create calendar events from natural language. Set reminders for important dates. Linen helps you stay organized without friction—just mention what you need to remember and Linen handles it intelligently.

👤 Personal Profile

Customize your experience with your name, pronouns, timezone. Get automatic birthday greetings and personalized responses that feel genuinely connected to who you are.

📱 Progressive Web App

Install on your phone, tablet, or desktop. It feels like a native app, works offline seamlessly, and runs fast with a beautiful, responsive UI.

And the best part? Everything stays on your device. Your memories never leave your computer or phone. There's no cloud, no servers analyzing your data, no algorithms tracking your behavior.

Linen is built on privacy-first principles: your data stays on your device, no one can access your deepest thoughts, and you have complete control—no tracking, no ads, no data collection, no corporate surveillance.

🔐 Confidentiality Guaranteed

All your most vulnerable moments, deepest fears, and darkest thoughts stay completely private. Your data is stored locally using IndexedDB—a secure, persistent database that lives only on your device. Your conversations and memories never leave your computer or phone. We literally cannot access your data. We don't have server logs of your conversations. No government agency, insurance company, or employer can see what you discuss with Linen. Your privacy is completely yours.

🎮 You Control Everything

Linen uses an affordable token system to power your AI experience. Each token gives you 20 AI messages. Your conversations and memories stay on your device. You're in control.

🌐 Works Offline

Full functionality without internet connection. Linen includes a built-in Local Assistant that provides intelligent responses completely offline. A Service Worker handles smart caching, so you can access your memories even without internet. No internet? No problem.

📊 No Tracking, No Ads

Zero data collection. Zero surveillance. Zero hidden tracking. We don't build profiles on you. We don't sell your data. We don't train models on your conversations. Your privacy is our priority.

💾 Export or Delete Anytime

Your data is genuinely yours. Export everything in a standard format and take it with you. Or delete it all with a single click. You're never locked in. You can leave anytime without losing your memories—they're yours to keep.

📋 Implemented Features

  • 🔄 Background Update Checks: Linen silently checks for updates in the background without disrupting your work. Adaptive check intervals respect your device's power and connection: 5 minutes on WiFi (plugged), 15 minutes on WiFi (battery), 1 hour on cellular (plugged), 4 hours on cellular (battery). When a new version is available, a gentle notification suggests closing and reopening the app to get the latest features.
  • 🎤 Voice Input: Talk to Linen using natural voice input. Your voice is processed locally with a clean, intuitive interface. Perfect for hands-free interaction when typing isn't convenient.
  • 💾 Smart Memory Management: Automatic memory detection and tagging from conversations. Intelligent memory surfacing to help you remember what matters.
  • 🎨 Beautiful, Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile with a thoughtful, minimal UI that respects your attention.

📋 Planned Enhancements

  • Cross-Device Sync: Optional encrypted cloud synchronization so your memories follow you across devices while remaining private
  • Advanced Memory Features: Pattern analysis to identify trends, mood tracking over time to see emotional patterns, and intelligent memory surfacing to resurface relevant past conversations
  • Collaboration Tools: Share specific conversations or memories with others while maintaining your privacy and control over what gets shared
  • Deeper AI Integration: Custom prompt templates for specialized workflows, multi-language support, and expanded voice capabilities
  • Community Features: Optional anonymous insight sharing to help improve Linen while protecting individual privacy

🎯 Technical Vision

Linen is built on cutting-edge web technologies: pure vanilla JavaScript (2000+ lines of well-organized, modular code), IndexedDB for local storage, Service Workers for offline functionality, and support for multiple AI providers through unified APIs. The architecture demonstrates that you don't need complex frameworks to build sophisticated applications.

Linen Privacy Policy

Your privacy is Linen's #1 priority.

What Data Linen Stores

  • Your name, email, pronouns, birthday (only if you add them)
  • Your conversations with Linen
  • Your calendar events and reminders
  • Your token balance
  • Nothing else.

Where Your Data Lives

  • 100% on YOUR device (your phone, your computer)
  • Linen cannot access it

What Linen Does Not Do

  • Never sells your data
  • Never shares your data with anyone
  • Never uses your data for ads or marketing
  • Never trains AI models on your conversations
  • Never accesses your device
  • Never tracks you
  • Never stores your data on any server

AI Conversations

  • When you use tokens for AI messages, your message is sent to an AI provider for processing
  • Only the message you send is transmitted — no personal data, no history
  • AI responses are stored locally on your device only

Data Deletion

  • "Clear All Data" button in Settings permanently deletes everything
  • No backups, no archives, no recovery
  • Your data is completely yours to delete

Policy Changes

  • If this policy ever changes, you'll be notified in-app
  • Changes will never reduce your privacy protections
